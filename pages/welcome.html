Welcome to DIU Lost & Found

DIU Lost & Found is a simple web-based platform designed to help students, faculty members, and staff report, publish, and recover lost items inside the university campus. This system makes the lost and found process faster, more organized, and easier to manage.

Users can browse published items, filter them by category, and submit information about found items through the public website. Every submitted item is reviewed by the management team before publication to ensure accuracy and security.

The management panel allows authorized administrators to manage categories, items, users, messages, and website content efficiently. This helps maintain a clear record of unclaimed items and improves communication between the finder and the rightful owner.

This project aims to create a smart, user-friendly, and reliable lost and found management system for Daffodil International University.

About Us

DIU Lost & Found is a web-based platform developed to help students, faculty members, and staff of Daffodil International University manage lost and found items in a simple and organized way. The system allows users to browse published items, submit found item information, and contact the management team for assistance.

Our goal is to make the process of recovering lost belongings faster, safer, and more efficient inside the university campus. Through the admin panel, authorized users can manage categories, items, user accounts, messages, and website content. This platform is designed to improve communication, maintain accurate records, and provide a user-friendly solution for campus lost and found management.
